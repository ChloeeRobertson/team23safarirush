The goal of the game is to get the Safari Jeep to exit the grid.

In order to get the Jeep to the exit the animals must be moved by clicking and dragging.

Animals can only be moved in the dimension they face. Now the 3x1 elephant can be moved.

The Jeep can now be moved to the exit.

Now you can choose next level, random level, or to save your score.

Score is based on time and number of moves.

With the bottom left button you can select any level. The bottom right button resets to board to its initial state. Top right button mutes the game.
